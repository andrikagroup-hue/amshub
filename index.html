Core Products & Services

Integrated Fire Protection Systems

We deliver reliable fire protection systems built to international standards — designed to protect lives, assets, and the future of your business.

1 / 16
01 — Detection & Alarm

Fire Detection & Alarm Systems

Advanced detection technology for early warning and fast response to minimize risk. Includes addressable FACP, smoke detectors, heat detectors, manual call points, and sirens.

1 / 4
02 — Suppression

Fire Suppression Systems

Automatic suppression systems using the latest technology for maximum protection. Available with a range of agents: FM-200, CO₂, clean agent, and inert gas systems.

1 / 4
03 — Hydrant & Pump

Hydrant & Pump Systems

High-performance hydrant and pump systems for reliable water distribution during emergencies. Designed to NFPA 20 and SNI standards for full operational reliability.

1 / 11
04 — Sprinkler

Sprinkler Systems

Automatic sprinkler systems designed for fast, effective fire control. Available in wet pipe, dry pipe, pre-action, and deluge configurations to suit your needs.
